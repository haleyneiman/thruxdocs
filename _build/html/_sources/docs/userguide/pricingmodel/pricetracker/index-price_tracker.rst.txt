.. _Price-Tracker:

Price Tracker
-------------

The price of the model is constantly being evaluated as the design changes.  Open the Price Tracker to view live cost impacts.

Shown below, note that the price as the location of an Electrical Equipment is shifted from one Room to another Room.

.. figure:: images/price_tracker_1.PNG
    :align: center

    Open the Price Tracker (dollar sign icon) by using the :ref:`Explorer Toolbar <Explorer-Toolbar>`

.. figure:: images/price_tracker_2.PNG
    :align: center

    Shifting a main distribution board to a different Room with the Price Tracker active

A tabular format can be viewed by opening the :ref:`Pricing Report <Pricing-Report>` Workspace.
